To play Game you have to enter row and column on your every turn.
1 X  |2    |3    |
4    |5    |6    |
7    |8    |9    |
Coordinates of boxes start from 0 row and 0 column like if you want to place your symbol(like X on 1st box) then you have to enter 0 row and 0 column or if you want to place your symbol on 9th index then you have to enter 2 row and 2 column.
